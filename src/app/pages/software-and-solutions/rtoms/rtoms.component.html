RTOMS, or Real Time Order Management System, allows users to place orders online seamlessly. RTOMS simplifies various user needs, such as managing orders in Excel sheets, making calls for orders, obtaining approvals from other parties, shift-wise ordering, online reporting, crate management, reimbursement, and more.

  • RTOMS is a one-stop solution for our users. By simply performing their assigned duties, users will find that it efficiently and effectively handles their daily tasks.
  • In RTOMS, we utilize a layered architecture where each layer has specific responsibilities to achieve the desired operations. This multilevel hierarchy incorporates abstraction at each layer.
  • RTOMS is an agile solution designed to support the growth of the dairy industry. It was developed with a focus on addressing the specific needs of dairy order management systems.
